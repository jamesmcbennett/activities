Activity Aggregation is a platform designed to help you track your progress in long-term races that span over months or even years. Your race position is displayed on an interactive map, continuously updated after each recorded activity.

To get started, explore the available races through the menu bar and contribute your own activities.

SwimYaleHarvard

Embark on a challenging swimming journey, starting at Yale's Gilder Boathouse, and navigate your way south to the Long Island Sound. Continue your aquatic adventure all the way around to Massachusetts Bay, where you'll eventually reach the Charles River, concluding your race at the Newell Boathouse at Harvard.

RunMarcoPolo

If running is more your style, take on the epic route of Marco Polo. This race commences in the historic city of Venice, Italy, and traces the ancient Silk Road until you reach the vibrant city of Beijing, China. It's a route once celebrated as the Grand Capital of the Yuan Dynasty during Kublai Khan's reign.
